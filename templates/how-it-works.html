How to Check EV Bike Rental Demand

Follow these simple steps to forecast bike demand in your area.

Step 1: Enter Date & Time

Provide the date and time when you want to predict the EV bike rental demand.

Step 2: Enter Weather Conditions

Input details like temperature, humidity, wind speed, and visibility to help the system make an accurate prediction.

Step 3: Select Additional Factors

Choose whether it's a holiday, the season, and if it's a functioning day for more refined predictions.

Step 4: Get the Prediction

Once you've filled in the details, click "Predict" to get the forecast for the EV bike demand for the chosen day and time.
